# Privacy Policy

This application is used to verify active OnlyFans subscribers and manage inbound Telegram access requests for a single creator account.

## What data is processed

- Telegram usernames, names, chat identifiers, and answers submitted to the bot
- The applicant's claimed OnlyFans username
- The applicant's stated spend range and purchase intent
- Connection-based subscriber status retrieved from OFAuth

## Why the data is processed

- To filter low-intent inquiries
- To verify whether a user is an active subscriber
- To decide whether Telegram access should be approved, delayed, or denied
- To review and expire access when subscriber status changes

## Data sharing

Data is used only for operating this creator access workflow. Credentials are not collected directly by this application. OnlyFans authentication is handled by OFAuth.

## Data retention

Operational review data may be retained in the bot state file for access control, reminders, and audit history until it is manually removed.

## Contact

For privacy questions related to this workflow, contact the creator through their official contact channel.
